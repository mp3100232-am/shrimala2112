Our Story

From the Farms to Your Table

Shrimala was born out of a simple desire: to provide a truly healthy snacking alternative without compromising on delicious flavors.

Fox nuts, or Makhana, have been a staple in Indian households for centuries. Recognizing their incredible nutritional profile—high in protein, rich in antioxidants, and naturally gluten-free—we set out to source the highest quality lotus seeds directly from organic farms in Bihar.

Every batch of Shrimala Makhana is carefully graded, perfectly roasted (never fried!), and seasoned with authentic, natural ingredients to ensure that every crunch is a guilt-free delight.
